9.What are the languages spoken on all cities named ‘Barcelona’ (you may need to join 3 tables =) )

mysql> SELECT country.name AS country, city.name AS city_name ,countryLanguage.language 
      ->FROM city
      ->JOIN country ON country.Code=city.CountryCode 
      ->JOIN countryLanguage ON countryLanguage.CountryCode=city.CountryCode 
      ->WHERE city.name='Barcelona';
